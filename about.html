About Our University

Pioneering excellence in higher education since 2005

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops the total person through cutting-edge teaching, research, and community service.

Our Vision

To be a world-class institution producing leaders who transform society through innovation and ethical values.

Our History

Established in 2005, Lead City University has grown from a modest beginning to become one of Nigeria's leading private universities. Licensed by the Federal Government and accredited by the National Universities Commission (NUC), we've consistently maintained the highest academic standards.
