Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Admission Requirements

  • Minimum of 5 credits in relevant subjects (including English and Mathematics) at not more than 2 sittings in WAEC/NECO/NABTEB or equivalent
  • UTME score of at least 180 (varies by program)
  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or sworn declaration of age
  • Letter of recommendation
